Privacy Policy (Dolar+)

This policy describes how information is handled in relation to the Dolar+ app.

Information Collected

The application uses analytics tools (such as Mixpanel) to collect anonymous usage data in order to improve the product. This may include in-app interaction events, device type, operating system version, and technical diagnostics.

The app does not collect personally identifiable information, account data, or financial transaction data from users.

Use of Information

Collected analytics data is used exclusively to understand feature usage, improve performance, and enhance the overall user experience.

Exchange rate data is obtained from publicly available APIs and is used solely to display market information inside the application.

Third-Party Services

The application uses third-party analytics services (such as Mixpanel). These services may collect technical identifiers such as device identifiers (e.g., IDFV) and IP address for analytics purposes.

Analytics data is not linked to the user's identity and is not used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

The application does not use advertising SDKs and does not perform tracking across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

User-entered ledger data is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to external servers.
